Welcome to the State of Arizona!


Welcome to the Grand Canyon State! is not only home to one of the world's natural wonders, but it has a rich history of different cultures, including Native American and Hispanic treasures. Arizona boasts an extremely diverse landscape, from mountains and pine trees in the north near the city of Flagstaff to the Sonoran deserts of the south. Rural, historical towns, along with some of the fastest growing metropolitan areas in the nation assure that Arizona offers something for everybody that chooses to visit. Incorporated as the 48th state in 1912, Arizona's spirit is as vast as its skies and as enduring as its rich, ancient history. Explore historic areas harkening back to the days of the Wild West, such as Tombstone. Whether you're here for the rich history, cultural diversity, or geographic beauty, Arizona has something to offer for all.
